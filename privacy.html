Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

CrossPost (referred to as "Multi Post" in the TikTok for Developers portal) is a self-hosted desktop application. When you run your own copy, all data stays under your control on your own machine. We (the creators of the software) do not operate a hosted service and do not collect any personal data from users.

Data Stored Locally by the App

  • Local user records (no real personal identifiers required).
  • Encrypted OAuth access and refresh tokens for platforms you connect (TikTok, etc.). Encryption uses AES-256-GCM with a key you control.
  • Post history, media, and scheduling data stored only in your local database or file system.

How OAuth Works

When you connect TikTok (or other platforms), the desktop app opens the official authorization page. After you approve the requested scopes, TikTok redirects back to a local callback in the app. We exchange the code for tokens locally and store only the encrypted tokens on your machine. We never see or log your TikTok password.

Data Sharing

The app only sends data to the official platform APIs you have explicitly authorized (for example, TikTok's Content Posting API to publish a video you chose to post).

Platform providers receive only what is necessary to perform the actions you request.

Your Rights

You can disconnect accounts at any time (this deletes the stored encrypted tokens). You can delete the entire app and all its data from your computer whenever you want.

Contact

Because this is self-hosted desktop software, privacy practices depend entirely on how you install and use it. You are responsible for your own data and compliance when operating the tool.

This policy applies to the CrossPost desktop software. The official site for the Multi Post TikTok integration is this repository / pages.
